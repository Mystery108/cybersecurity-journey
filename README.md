## 💡 Success Tips

### 🔥 The Formula That Works

```
1. Consistency (1-2 hours daily)
   ↓
2. Hands-on Learning (70% labs, 30% theory)
   ↓
3. Building Projects (portfolio > certs early)
   ↓
4. Documenting Learning (blogs, writeups, repos)
   ↓
5. Networking (LinkedIn, Twitter, communities)
   ↓
6. Getting Hired (₹15-20 LPA offer)
```

### ✅ Do's
- ✅ Start with Phase 1, do one subtopic/day
- ✅ Build projects alongside learning
- ✅ Share progress on LinkedIn
- ✅ Join CTF communities (HackTheBox, TryHackMe)
- ✅ Write technical blogs about your learning
- ✅ Practice explaining concepts to others

### ❌ Don'ts
- ❌ Don't try to memorize everything
- ❌ Don't skip practical labs
- ❌ Don't wait for "perfect" understanding
- ❌ Don't build projects only at the end
- ❌ Don't apply for jobs without a portfolio
- ❌ Don't isolate - join communities
